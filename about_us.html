Our Mission

Black Belt Excellence

The goal of martial arts is to master one's self and to obtain balance mentally, physically, and spiritually. We call this journey Black Belt Excellence. It is important to notice the word journey. Black Belt Excellence is not a place, rather it is a direction. This journey is not about fighitng, but about living -- that is, the pricinples students learn provide guidance for overall self-improvement. The five elements of confidence, respect, discipline, control, and physical health are vital to martial arts expertise. As you attain self-mastery of each element, your mind, body, and spirit converge, and you will grow to a higher self-realization. Not only do students become black belts in martial arts, but they strive to become academic black belts and eventaully, corporate black belts or black belt employees. We use "black belt" as a metaphor for personal excellence.


Our Philosophy

Improving the mind, body, and spirit is an intricate yet simple process. Self-mastery is acquired through HABITS! We as individuals have the ability to nurture our nature. In other words, we can make or break what is natural to us -- habits. Repetition is the key, but remember what you repeat is what you master. Therefore only repeat what is correct. If a student practices sloppy techniques, then he or she will become a master of sloppy techniques. Practice does not make perfect. Perfect practice makes perfect! New nature can only be developed through concentration and focus. The mind, body, and spirit must train in harmony. Wherever your body is, so should your mind and spirit be as well. The philosophy is simple. Eliminate each bad habit, and replace it with a good habit. Just remember:


Wherever you are, BE THERE.


Our Style

American Goju

Our style, American Goju, is a transcendent of Okinawan Goju-ryu with added emphasis from Shotokan, Tae Kwon Do, and American boxing. Goju (Hard/Soft) is based on the same philosophy as Yin/Yang -- that is our opposites give us harmony in the universe. This is also the fundamental law of physics: For every action there is an equal and opposite reaction. Dark would mean nothing without light. We balance our strengths by focusing on our weaknesses. Courage needs fear, and fear needs courage. At OKS our primary goal is to help individuals discover and attain personal balance.
