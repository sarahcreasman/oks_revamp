Getting Started

You or your child will be able to participate in class while receiving one-on-one aid from an instructor as needed. You will also have the opportunity to talk with the instructor or staff about any concerns or questions you may have.


To schedule your intro lesson you may call us at (502)-762-1515 or email us at olympickarate@bellsouth.net.


Try to arrive 5-10 minutes early if it's your first time at the dojo so that we can introduce ourselves and show you around our facility. Be sure you or your child comes dressed in comfortable/flexible clothes.


Come by the dojo for a FREE introductory lesson!
